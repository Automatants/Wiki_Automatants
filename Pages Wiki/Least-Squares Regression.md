# Least-Squares regression

The Least-Squares regression is a type of [[Linear Regression]] where the [[Loss Function]] is the sum of of the squares :

$$ J(\theta) = \frac{1}{2} \sum_{i=1}^N  (\theta^T x^{(i)} - y^{(i)})^2 $$
